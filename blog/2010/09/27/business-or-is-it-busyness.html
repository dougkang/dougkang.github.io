Business or is it Busyness?

  • Blog
  • Projects

27 Sep 2010

I’ve been trying to keep myself busy in a number of things in order to take my mind off certain other things.  I realize how much happier I am when there are things to do.  Of course, being incredibly busy is not good either - just enough to get your mind off the little, mundane things.  You know, enough work where you don’t have time to be proud or arrogant.  Because no matter how important things seem now, some things, in the grander scheme of the world, they just don’t matter.  There are definitely times where you just have to take a step back, introspect, retrospect, and prospect.  But sometimes - you know, when the crap really hits the fan - you just have to put your head down and work your butt off until the dust settles a bit.
