what this Blog is About

13 Feb 2010

So here it is. The beginning. It’s so hard to start these things sometimes, so lets just cut the awkwardness now.

The reason I am starting this blog is mainly to document my own life as I enter into a new chapter. I have no idea what this blog is actually going to turn out to be. Maybe I’m going to all get deep and personal in this blog? Gross, probably not. Maybe I should document my my first steps into the professional world of Computer Science? But then again, the world doesn’t need another techie blog.

Most likely, this is going to be where I write my random thoughts/ideas/crap that someday I can muck through later and reflect, regret, or feel nostalgic.

Let’s see how things turn out, shall we?
