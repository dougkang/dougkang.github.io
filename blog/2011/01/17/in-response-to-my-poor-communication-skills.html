In response to my poor communication skills

  • Blog
  • Projects

17 Jan 2011

I can no longer ignore the fact that my communication skills are quite poor,  my main concern being the inability to formulate my thoughts into clear and concise words.  I may have been able to get away with poor communication skills in college, but in the world of industry, writing and speaking in a clear, concise manner are necessities.  It would be an enormous disrespect to the people I interact with if I chose not to develop these two abilities.

To try and combat my lack of ability, I will use this blog as an outlet to lucidly write down my thoughts and machinations.  Just a simple writing exercise here, folks.  You are welcome to join in my journey to better communication and just as welcome to leave.
