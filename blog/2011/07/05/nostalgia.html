05 Jul 2011

As I walked to the library this morning, I couldn’t help but feel nostalgic.  One whiff of “old book smell” - you know, the slightly acrid, pungent smell that seems ubiquitous of all libraries - and I was whisked back into the memories of my collegiate career.  Ah, the sandbox of life - the last step before, ready or not, one ventures into “the real world” where the big kids play.  Ironically, from the beginning of my four years I was pining for a taste of the real world, so I am inclined to think that this feeling of nostalgia is one of those “grass is greener” moments.
