22 Jun 2011

As of late, I have been begrudgingly working on increasing my vocabulary for the GRE. With less than 5 fortnights away from my test-taking date, I have thus far been unfettered in my quest to memorize ~50 words daily - though in the back of my mind I am counting the days before my verve wanes. I hope that the upshot of this drudgery is, at least, a decent score. Memorizing words is such a travail.
